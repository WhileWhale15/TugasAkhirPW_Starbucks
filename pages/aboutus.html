It's Starbucks

Starbucks Coffee Indonesia opened its first outlet at Plaza Indonesia on May 17, 2002. By January 2018, the franchise had expanded to 326 branches across 22 major cities in Indonesia. PT Mitra Adi Perkasa Tbk (MAPI) oversees the Starbucks franchise in Indonesia.
The global Starbucks brand originated in 1971 in a small shop at Seattle's Pike Place Market, focusing on offering high-quality, fresh-roasted coffee beans. The name "Starbucks" draws inspiration from Moby Dick, reflecting the romance of the open sea and the coffee trading traditions of ancient times.
